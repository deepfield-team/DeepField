## SPE 10 – MODEL 2

This folder contains reservoir model SPE 10 – MODEL 2 presended in the SPE paper "Tenth SPE comparative solution project: a comparison of upscaling techniques"
by M. Christie and M. Blunt, SPE, 2001.
